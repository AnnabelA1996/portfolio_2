M.M Linen Marketing and Graphic Collateral

As the in house Graphic Designer and Marketing Assistant at M.M Linen, I produced a variety of digital content for their social media profiles, EDM, newsletters, and website. As well as this, I also created all printed graphics material for their seasonal catalogues, product swing tags, magazine advertisements, packging, and instore signage and window banners. My responsibilities as the graphic designer here, was to stay true to and own the brand identity, follow brand guidelines, bring new and on-trend ideas to the table while also maintaining solid and cohesive graphics that functioned to keep the brand and their products at the forefront of the market.
