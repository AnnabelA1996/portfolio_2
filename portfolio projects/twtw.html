To Watch The Water Exhibition Collateral

To Watch the Water was a collaborative photographic exhibition by Megan Hopkins and Ella Hickford held at The Corner Store in Christchurch. For this I was asked to create collateral including, vinyl wall labels, posters for advertising, pamphlets outlining the details of works that were up for sale, and an exhibition catalogue discussing the concepts of each of the artists bodies of work and how they fused together for this exhibition.
