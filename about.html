About

Hey, I’m Annabel Ambrosius… Before you ask, yes, my surname is like the dessert, the type of apple and the stuff Greek gods were drunk on 24/7 – Let’s get to the important stuff!

I have always been a creative person and am lucky enough to be able to translate this strength into my career. Having studied a Bachelor of Fine Arts majoring in Graphic Design at the University of Canterbury and graduating in 2019, I learnt the ropes of everything design and more. I have since found myself designing web, packaging, products, marketing and social media collateral for wholesale products. As well as this I have tried my hand at creating branding and business cards for shiny new projects, business rebrands, and freelance photographers.

More recently I have been challenging myself to further develop my skills in the web arena by completing a Web Training Package through Yoobee Colleges. Here I have focussed my skills on web development and learning coding languages HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Needless to say, I like a challenge and am up for any task that comes my way, design, web or otherwise! I thoroughly enjoy problem solving. Marrying this passion with my desire to be embedded in the world of visual communications and marketing is something I have loved developing both professionally and outside the 9 – 5. My core values as a designer centre around uncovering creative solutions that best fit the needs of the task at hand, but with a fun and outside of the box approach.

Aside from graphic design, my interests still largely lie within the creative realm. I enjoy sewing, painting (pictures, not houses), singing and playing piano. I do also enjoy getting out in nature in summer and going for a dip in the ocean.
